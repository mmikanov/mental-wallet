# Requirements Document

## Introduction

Badges and achievements add gamification to encourage consistent practice. Users earn badges for streaks, variety, and total usage milestones. Badges are displayed on cards and a dedicated achievements page, and can be shared socially.

## Glossary

- **Badge**: A gamification reward earned for consistent practice, variety, or milestones.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Badges and Achievements

**User Story:** As a user, I want to earn badges for consistent practice and variety, so that I feel rewarded for building healthy habits.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE App SHALL award Streak Badges when a user achieves 7-day and 30-day consecutive streaks on any single card.
2. THE App SHALL award Variety Badges when a user completes cards in 5 different categories and when completing every available category.
3. THE App SHALL award Consistency Badges at milestones: 10 uses of any single card, 50 total uses, and 100 total uses.
4. WHEN a Badge is earned, THE App SHALL display an in-app notification or animation acknowledging the new Badge.
5. THE App SHALL display earned Badges on the Focused_Card view (card-specific badges) and on a dedicated achievements page (all badges).
6. WHEN a user taps a Badge on the achievements page, THE App SHALL offer a "Share this achievement" action generating a pre-filled social media post with #MentalHealthWallet.
